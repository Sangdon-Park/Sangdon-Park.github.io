About Me

I am a Post Doctoral Researcher at KAIST, specializing in innovative AI-driven interactive systems with expertise in natural language processing and computational dialogue frameworks. My research focuses on creating immersive character systems that adapt to user behavior, enhancing narrative experiences through advanced AI integration.
