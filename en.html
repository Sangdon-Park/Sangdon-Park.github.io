About

Biography

Education and Career

I received my B.S./M.S. in Mathematical Sciences and Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from KAIST. My work spans smart grid, edge computing, optimization, and AI-driven system design. Since March 1, 2026, I serve as Assistant Professor at Daejeon University.

Current Focus

My current agenda focuses on robust LLM deployment, trustworthy AI, and practical AI software engineering. I combine mathematical modeling, empirical evaluation, and production-grade implementation.
