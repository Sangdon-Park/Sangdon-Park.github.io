About: Immersed in AI

For the past few months, I have been deeply immersed in the possibilities of AI Large Language Models (LLMs). Utilizing cutting-edge models like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini, I explored their limitations and potential across the entire process of research, planning, and development. It was the most intense period of focus in my life, where I pushed myself to maximize AI's potential, even sacrificing sleep.

As a result, I was able to complete the Edge Computing GUI Simulator, a project I had desired for four years. Additionally, the AI Character Conversation Project, started as a promise to my daughter, revealed unexpected fun and potential, opening up a new path in game development through interactions with characters that felt alive, going beyond simple chatting.

Actively using AI coding assistants like Cursor, I experienced astonishing productivity, sometimes amidst confusion. The speed at which ideas turned into reality was incomparable. With this conviction, I am scheduled to join Sayberry Games soon, dedicating myself to R&D for creating new AI-based interactive experiences. This 3-month immersion brought growth equivalent to the past 10 years, leading to the successful proposal of an 8-month, ~$220K (300M KRW) AI game development project.

I wish to share this valuable experience and know-how with more people, rather than keeping it to myself. (Please refer to the 'AI Seminars & Talks' section below for more details).
